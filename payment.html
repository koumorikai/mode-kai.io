Payment by card on the website

You can pay for your order online with a Visa or MasterCard bank card. Payment by payment
cards is carried out in the following way:

  • when placing an order on the website, you will be prompted to choose a payment method.
  • in the column "Payment" you need to select "Visa/MasterCard".
  • after that, you will be redirected to the page of the bank's secure payment system, where you will need to confirm the payment.

We do not work with postpaid.

International delivery

Delivery abroad is carried out with the help of Ukrposhta and Nova Poshta, the cost of delivery
is also paid when placing an order.

In some cases, the purchase price may be paid separately, please pay attention to the relevant information when placing an order.

The order processing period can be 10-20 days. After registration, you will be sent a letter to
e-mail with a tracking code for tracking (only applies to international shipments).
